- Cost coverage: free, paid, varies, and unknown

The accepted set is intentionally smaller than 24. Candidates were deferred where a current primary source, credible coordinates, safe public access, or a reasonable ≤25-minute station connection could not all be established confidently. Nothing was added merely to reach a count.

Deferred coverage includes Pulinchodu, Companypady, Ambattukavu, Muttom, Kalamassery, Cochin University, Kaloor, Town Hall, Elamkulam, Thykoodam, Petta, Vadakkekotta, and SN Junction. A future reverification pass should prioritise official municipal, institutional, venue, or tourism sources for these stations.

All walking times are conservative planning estimates based on mapped venue and verified station locations. They are never presented as official rail or walking-route measurements. No exact prices or permanent opening hours are stored. Accessibility notes remain null because authoritative, current accessibility evidence was not consistently available.

Primary sources include Kerala Tourism, Ernakulam District administration, Kerala Museum, LuLu Mall Kochi, Centre Square, Oberon Mall, Regional Sports Centre Kochi, and official venue or government pages. Coordinates and proximity were cross-checked with map pages based on OpenStreetMap or Google Maps coordinate searches.

The public seed contains no source URLs. Source evidence is written to `exploreSourceRecords` for enabled administrators only.
